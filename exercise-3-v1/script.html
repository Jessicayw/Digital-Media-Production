Work Life Balance

Main point: Highlight the relaxing vacation

Short Description:

A lady was busy in working and felt tried. Then she backed home for the vacation and enjoyed time with friends. She felt on her feet again.

  6. Wide shot:The lady watched the firework show with friends.
  7. Close shot:The lady feel on her feet again.
  8. Close shot:The lady go back to the city and work with energy.
